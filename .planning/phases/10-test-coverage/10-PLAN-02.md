Key implementation notes:

**stealPokemon():**
- The method takes pokemon FROM the trainer's team (Team Rocket steals from the trainer).
- Requires `ModalQueueService` spy for the `teamRocketFailsModal` path.
- With team >= 2 and no escape-rope: the component sets `auxPokemonList = trainerTeam`, pushes 'steal-pokemon' and 'select-from-pokemon-list', then calls `finishCurrentState()` → current state becomes 'select-from-pokemon-list'.
- With team < 2: opens `teamRocketFailsModal` then calls `doNothing()`.

**tradePokemon():**
- Pushes 'trade-pokemon' onto the stack.
- With team.length === 1: sets `currentContextPokemon = team[0]` (not `auxPokemonList`).
- With team.length > 1: sets `auxPokemonList = team`, pushes 'select-from-pokemon-list'.

**handleRareCandyEvolution():**
- Only acts when `getPokemonThatCanEvolve().length > 0`.
- Calls `gameStateService.repeatCurrentState()` then `trainerService.removeItem(rareCandy)` then `chooseWhoWillEvolve('rare-candy')`.
- If no evolvable pokemon: does nothing (no spy calls).
